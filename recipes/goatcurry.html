Goat Curry Recipe

Description

Looking for an easy Punjabi Mutton Curry recipe? Follow this step-by-step recipe to make flavour-packed Punjabi Mutton Curry at home!

Ingredients

  • 500 gm mutton
  • 2 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 4 onions
  • 2 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 4 clove
  • 5 green cardamom
  • 1 cinammon stick
  • salt as required
  • 2 cups water
  • 4 tablespoon ghee
  • 1/1 cup yogurt
  • 2 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 3 tomato
  • 8 peppercorn
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder

Steps

  1. Cook the whole spices with onion and ginger-garlic paste
  2. Add the powdered spices tomatoes
  3. Add the mutton pieces and yogurt
  4. Ready to be served
